About the Artist/Song

At 26 years old, (Navy vet)Zack Bryan released his breakthrough album American Heartbreak. It was certified gold in less than six months, debuing at No. 5 on the Billboard 200.

"Something in the Orange" was a No.1 country hit. It rose to No. 10 on the Billboard Hot 100

“Something in the Orange” explores the emotional aftermath of a relationship that is clearly ending, even as the narrator struggles to accept it. While the song opens with reassurance and warmth, that comfort slowly gives way to uncertainty and longing.

Artist Insight

“Everyone thinks it was over some deep, dark thing, and it was just me in a cabin in Wisconsin. I thought about the word ‘orange’ and I was watching the sunset and I was like, ‘Oh, that’s a cool story to tell in a song.’”

-Zack Bryan
